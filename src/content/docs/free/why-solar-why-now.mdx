## How To Use This

Treat the chart as context, not a quote. The useful homeowner question is whether your current bill, utility rules, roof, and project price make solar valuable enough for your goals.

## Next Action

Use the calculator with your approximate monthly kWh and a realistic solar offset to ground your expectations before you talk to anyone. Prefer a guided walkthrough? Read [Why Solar, Why Now](https://solarpower101.github.io/learn/why-solar-why-now/) on the SolarPower101 platform.
